Questions

Frequently asked

Is spiritual coach certification worth it?

Yes, obtaining a spiritual life coach certification can be incredibly worthwhile. It provides structured training, credibility, proven methods, and the confidence to effectively guide clients on their spiritual journey. Certification from a reputable program like Awakened Academy demonstrates your commitment and expertise, helping you attract clients and build a fulfilling coaching practice that aligns with your values. It's an investment in both your personal growth and your professional success.

How long does it take to become a certified spiritual life coach?

The time varies depending on the program and your pace. Our comprehensive online Spiritual Life Coach Certification is designed to be completed in about 3–6 months on a fast track, 6–12 months at a typical pace, or up to 18 months fully self-paced.

What can I do as a certified spiritual life coach?

You can empower individuals to connect with their inner wisdom, discover their purpose, overcome challenges, and live a more meaningful, spiritually aligned life. Graduates run private coaching practices, author books, lead retreats, teach meditation, and build online courses.

How soon can I start?

You can begin today. Book a discovery call to confirm fit and get started, enrollment opens monthly with only a limited number of spots.

Is it self-paced, or do I have to show up for lots of events?

The curriculum is 100% self-paced so you can move at your own speed. In addition, you get twice-monthly live group calls with the founders for live Q&A and personal guidance.

What support do I get?

Twice-monthly live sessions where you connect directly with the founders and ask questions, plus ongoing email support from our team. Every certification student also gets 1-on-1 access to Michael as part of the program.

How do I know if spiritual life coaching is right for me?

Spiritual life coaching is one of the most fulfilling careers in the world. Who wouldn't want to be paid to expand their own personal and spiritual growth while helping others do the same? On your discovery call we'll walk through your situation, what you're called to, and whether this work is the right fit.

How many hours per week should I commit?

The Academy is self-paced, you decide how many hours to put in each week. Consider what is realistic for your life, and go for that. Many students dedicate 3–8 hours per week and complete the program comfortably alongside work or family commitments.

Do you offer payment plans?

Absolutely. We offer several payment plan options. We'll walk through them with you on your discovery call to find one that fits your situation.

How much is the tuition?

Tuition ranges from $3,000 to $9,000+ depending on the level of support you choose. Payment plans are available. We'll go through the options with you on your discovery call.

Do I have access to the founder, Michael?

Yes. You receive 1-on-1 access to Michael as part of the program, plus twice-monthly live group calls where you can ask questions, receive deep insights, and get feedback directly from Michael.

Can I do this program while working a full-time job or being a full-time parent?

Yes. Many of our students are full-time workers, students, and parents. A core part of what you'll learn in the Academy is how to simplify your life and create new habits, so the program itself becomes an exercise in the time and energy work you'll later teach your own clients.

I'm already a coach, will this be repetitive?

No. This is a one-of-a-kind training that will enhance your current practice. You'll be able to go deeper with your clients, integrate spiritual transformation into your existing methodology, and access the inner work that most coaching trainings skip entirely.

Does Awakened Academy come with a guarantee?

We offer a 14-day refund window from program start. If the program isn't the right fit, you can request a full refund within that period. After 14 days, no refunds. We've found that students who are fully committed get the most from the work, so we ask you to weigh your decision carefully on your discovery call before enrolling.
