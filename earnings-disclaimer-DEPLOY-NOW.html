Three Layers of Transformation at Awakened Academy

Our programs are designed to transform your life on three levels.

Layer One

You become who you are meant to be

You discover your dharma. You release what has weighed you down. You upgrade your relationship with money, worth, and your own gifts. You build a daily practice that grounds you for life. This is the foundation of the work you do for others, because we believe the more you grow, the deeper you can help others. Personal transformation comes before service.

Many students remain at this layer for years and tell us this work alone has been worth more than the cost of the program — even if they never go on to coach professionally. The personal transformation here is yours forever: for you, your family, and everyone in your life. We encourage our students to keep doing this deep inner work and developing themselves so they are always growing.

Layer Two

You become a light for the people in your life

Once you learn how to help yourself and others, you can begin serving people. You can start helping your family, friends and community — even before you get paid clients — because as the saying goes, "charity begins at home."

To be of service right away, you can offer free coaching, lead meditations, host circles, write, teach, share. None of this requires paid clients or marketing. It only requires you, showing up with what you have learned. This practice and commitment to serve is what opens the doors to paid clients and a full practice. We believe it's better to just start now, than to wait until you are "perfect" and have everything set up. Practice makes perfect. Start now.

Layer Three

You build a practice that pays you to be yourself and share your gifts

Once you feel ready, you can choose to become a paid coach. When you are ready, we walk this path with you, step by step — helping you figure out your dharma, the ideal way you can share your gifts, and get paid.

This process does require time, commitment and effort, and we encourage you to enjoy every moment and celebrate every success. The journey is the destination.

The Honest Truth About Coaching, Money, and Results

We want you to walk this path with eyes wide open. Here is what actually happens in students' lives.

Among the students who genuinely engage with the program, the range of outcomes is wide:

  • Some sign their first paying client within weeks. Others take months or longer.
  • Some build beautiful part-time practices serving a handful of clients they love. Some build strong six-figure annual practices. A few of our top graduates run multi-six-figure businesses.
  • Some take what they learn inward and never coach professionally, but profoundly transform their own lives and the lives of those around them. We see this as a fully valid and meaningful outcome.

We also want to be honest about another group: students who join and don't engage at all. They sign up but don't show up. They get busy with other things. They don't watch the trainings, attend the live calls, or apply what they learn. Despite every form of support, opportunity, and encouragement we provide, we cannot make anyone do the work. Some students simply don't take action. Everyone is different. That is the honest truth of it.

The students whose lives transform share certain qualities. They take personal responsibility for their results. They face their inner blocks rather than running from them. They serve people generously, often beginning with friends, family, and free clients. They course-correct when something is not working. They do not wait for results to find them.

The students whose lives do not transform tend to wait for the program to do the work for them, stay in patterns they have not yet faced, look for shortcuts that do not exist, care more about what they will receive than what they will give, or simply never engage at all.

We cannot guarantee any individual outcome. No honest school can. What we can offer is a complete vehicle for transformation, the most comprehensive support we know how to provide, and our continued presence for as long as you walk this path with us.

How We Came to This Work

After a spiritual awakening at age 18, Michael felt called to share what he had experienced. He spent the next decade teaching meditation and pioneering a form of spiritual life coaching for free — long before the term "spiritual life coaching" existed. He did it because he loved it, and because he could see how much it helped people.

After ten years of giving this work away, Michael wanted to dedicate his life to it without needing a separate job to pay the bills. After many months of deep meditation in India, he returned to the UK and began building a sustainable practice around what he had been quietly teaching for years. Eventually he moved to Kauai, Hawaii to pursue this dream full time.

Through synchronicities and many challenges, he figured out how to run a thriving full-time business doing what he loved — coaching clients all over the world, sharing courses and meditations, and earning a real living from his gifts. It was around this time that he met Arielle, who arrived as if by perfect timing to deepen the practice and help more souls.

Once Michael and Arielle had cracked the code on how to make this work — how to serve people deeply, build a practice that supported a beautiful life, and turn spiritual gifts into a real livelihood — people from all over the world began emailing: How did you do this? Please teach me your methods.

They meditated on this and recognized it as a calling. To make the biggest impact, they would shift from their personal coaching practices to teaching the teachers. They had figured it out for themselves first. Now they could honestly help others figure it out, too.

That is how Awakened Academy was born. Everything in this program is built on what we lived through, learned, and tested in our own lives before we ever taught it to anyone else.
