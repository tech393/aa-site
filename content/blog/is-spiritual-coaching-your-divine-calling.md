## What Is Spiritual Coaching?

Spiritual coaching blends intuition, inner wisdom, and transformation. Unlike therapy or traditional coaching, practitioners help others remember their authentic selves through healing, divine guidance, and soul reconnection.

## Signs You Might Be a Spiritual Coach in Disguise

- You sense people's energy intuitively
- You're experiencing or completed your own awakening
- You desire work that transforms lives, not just finances

## The Evolution of a Healer: From Wounded to Wise

## FAQ

**Q:** Do I need to be "fully healed" to be a spiritual coach?
A: Healing is lifelong. Honesty, personal work, and love-centered leadership matter more than perfection.

**Q:** Can I really make a living doing this work?
A: Yes. With proper support, strategy, and alignment, earning a sustainable income is achievable.

**Q:** What if I'm afraid to put myself out there?
A: Fear is normal; courage means acting despite it. Your message outweighs your fear.

**Q:** How do I know I'm not just imagining this calling?
A: If it won't go away, it's real. Persistent whispers signal authentic soul connection.

**Q:** What are the first steps to begin?
A: Begin with daily presence and practice, then seek mentorship, community, or formal training.

**Q:** How do I stay consistent and avoid burnout?
A: Start small and sustainable. Establish boundaries, maintain simple practices, avoid overcommitment, prioritize rest, reconnect with purpose regularly.
