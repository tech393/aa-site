**What to do when you don’t like your job or life, need money and you don’t know what to do about it?**

## In this audio, you will discover

by **Michael Mackintosh** | Awakened Academy

## No matter what you’re going through, you can turn it around.

First of all, it’s important to not feel bad about feeling bad as that only makes it worse. Krishnamuri said, “It is no measure of health to be well adjusted to a profoundly sick society”. This means that if you’re feeling stressed out about your situation, it’s an appropriate response to the situation you are in. It’s ok to feel stressed and anxious when you don’t have the means to meet your basic needs.  You may feel worried about paying bills, buying food, eating enough and having a life that matters. The good news is you can turn this all around using the methods in this article.

## I was nearly evicted and turned it around

I’ve been on the edge more than I’d like to admit. I understand what it feels like to have no money and feel confused, lost, worried about not paying rent and getting kicked out. On top of that, not eating enough food leads us to not think properly which only makes it worse. However, I have to admit that it’s been these situations that have forced me to change myself and my life and break free. I remember one time that I had no money and was worried I’d be evicted. By using the methods below I figured out not only how to pay my rent on time, but more importantly, I figured out a way to get out of the low income trap I was in that lead me to the situation. That situation forced me to make more money overall and be free from worry. So the pain of loss ended up being a blessing that helped make me wealthier.

This situation is a GIFT in disguise. Life gives us what we need in the moment (even if it’s not what we want). There really is a secret benefit in this situation and it’s helping you get to the next level. Instead of thinking it’s a problem you shouldn’t have, start asking yourself.

WHAT CAN I LEARN FROM THIS SITUATION?

HOW CAN I PROFIT FROM THIS SITUATION?

WHAT IS THIS SITUATION FORCING ME TO DO OR CHANGE?

HOW CAN I USE THIS SITUATION TO TURN MY LIFE AROUND FOR THE BETTER?

When you flip the situation around and start seeing it as a blessing in disguise, and a gift, you can start to relax.

*I am in the perfect place at the perfect time.*

*I can benefit from this. This situation is making me better.*

Make note of any ideas that come into your mind after asking these questions. Keep asking them until you get some new ideas that will support you.

I can’t tell you how much this mantra has made a difference in my life. When you truly surrender to whatever is the highest good in life, you come out of ego and go into a new state of power and freedom. The ego thinks it knows what’s best and has a plan about what it wants to happen in life. But the ego is limited, narcissistic and has no idea about millions of awesome things outside of its little window that it looks through into the world. In fact, there are so many possibilities, it’s mind-blowing.

When you truly are surrendered to whatever is the greatest benefit for you and others, you come into a zone of power where the magic happens and new opportunities open up.

It was after I truly surrendered to the highest good that I received money and ideas to pay my bills and get out of a dire financial situation. That money and those ideas were NOT the things I was normally thinking about. If I’d stayed in the confines of my ego I would have remained trapped, broke and unable to see a way out.

The key here is to deeply surrender. This means to let go and release control and be open to stand in a ray of power, like a beam of light where you surrender your life, your mind, your body and your wealth to doing, thinking and using your money on the things that are truly the highest good for all. This means you will act in NEW ways and open to new ideas.

It may mean getting a new job. It may mean moving to a new home. It may mean doing a spiritual practice, calling someone, working on a project, doing a talk or all kinds of things. But to be surrendered means to be willing to choose differently and act in new ways. It feels liberating and rejuvenating.

I get it. When you’re stressed and anxious (and don’t have extra time or money) food can become the last thing on the list. But if we don’t eat right, we can’t think clearly or act effectively. We become irrational, over-emotional and can cause ourselves a lot of problems. I made this mistake many times and would just spin out with low blood sugar and wonder why nothing changed. One day I decided to go to the store and invest in food to fuel my thinking and creativity. I couldn’t believe how much better I felt once I had enough to eat and my blood sugar levels stabilized! It was like going from living in a dilapidated factory trying to write down my ideas on a blackboard in the dark, to entering into a beautiful, clean, well-lit creative studio with everything I could need to create million dollar plans and them implement them.

We need fuel to function properly. Eat well. Ideally, foods that don’t give you a blood sugar crash (avoid white sugars and refined carbs etc.).

When you’ve drank enough water and eaten well, you’ll feel much more capable to change things in your life.

** **When we’re stressed we can feel like there is no time for rest and relaxation. But being in a panic and running around like a headless chicken is not a good idea. In fact, when we look back on all that frantic thinking and spinning, it’s pretty obvious that it’s more of a problem than a solution. To get inspired ideas and to act effectively, we need to relax and rest well. All athletes know that if they don’t eat right and get adequate rest, they won’t be able to perform at their highest potential. To avoid rest is not smart, it’s self-sabotage.

Go to sleep earlier.
Have baths with sea salts and essential oils
Do meditation or yoga
Read a book

The more you feel relaxed and peaceful, the better everything is.

The previous steps are all designed to help you feel better and open your genius mind up to new ideas. Once you have an idea of what you want, you can then use the power of your mind to create change.

For example, if you want more money and time, you can simply use this method to find the next steps to make it happen.

You can do this while you’re in the bath or while you’re dozing off to sleep feeling peaceful. The important thing here is to feel good about this vision. See it as a fun game, something you do just for fun to make you feel better. If you don’t feel good about your vision, then stop. Change the vision or your attitude towards it until you do feel good about it. The key thing is to imagine this new reality as if it’s happening now, in the present and feel happy, content and strong about it.

Your subconscious mind is a manifesting machine. When you hold a vision in your mind with feeling, it gives an order to your subconscious mind to make it so. This means a deeper part of you will begin working towards achieving that vision, it will scan the entire, vast resources of all your experiences, connections, possibilities and tune into a situation you have no idea about to bring forth ideas that will support the unfoldment of that desire.

The more you surrender to the highest good for all and hold the vision of what you want, the more ideas and subtle insights will emerge in your conscious mind. Take note! You may find yourself looking online for new jobs, you may feel compelled to offer a free talk, to reach out to an old friend or go into a shop.  Follow the guidance, you never know how these ideas may lead you to something else, which lead to something else and then all of a sudden you’ve figured it all out and got what you wanted. Life is a bizarre adventure and our journey doesn’t go in a straight line. It’s wiggly, wobbly and weird. Once you accept this, you can go with the new ideas more easily and trust in the process.

Once you have clear ideas on what to do, take action NOW. Don’t wait around. Make the call. Go for the job interview. Advertise your work. Get the client. Do what is needed. To make a change, we need a shift in mindset *and* in action. But not action in the sense of just running around like a headless chicken. Act from a place of inspiration and power. Try new things, explore new opportunities.

Once you know what you want, take action, and keep moving towards you goal. It’s actually quite fun when you get on board and take command of your life.

Also, the more you eat well, take baths, go for walks, rest and renew in these types of ways, the more you’ll get ideas and guidance to make sure you’re on the right track with your actions.

## What will happen if you do this?

Once you do this, you will feel better right now and begin to feel hope and even excitement. You’ll tap into the magic of life, and embrace your own inner power. You’ll begin to notice new opportunities in your life and sooner or later, find yourself in a NEW situation and look back on this time with a smile.

Things are getting better for you!
