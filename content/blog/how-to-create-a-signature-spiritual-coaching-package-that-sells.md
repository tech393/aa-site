## Spiritual Coaching Packages vs. Hourly Sessions

The core argument is that package-based coaching creates deeper client commitment than hourly rates.

> When you sell hours, you are selling time. When you sell a package, you are selling a result.

Three primary benefits:

- Deeper client commitment through higher financial investment
- Predictable, sustainable income for coaches
- Holistic support including asynchronous communication and custom materials

## How to Choose Your Spiritual Coaching Niche First

Specificity matters. Rather than serving everyone, coaches should identify the intersection of their unique gifts and client transformation needs. Examples include intuitive healers addressing ancestral trauma or mindset coaches focused on spiritual entrepreneurs.

## Why Your Signature Package Changes Everything

A signature package provides:

- Energetic clarity through structured transformation
- Premium pricing reflecting the depth of work
- Client commitment via clear expectations
- Consistent, measurable results
- Attraction of aligned clients

## Step 1, Define Your Unique Transformation

Discovery questions guide coaches to identify specific pain points and desired outcomes. Transformation examples include:

- "From People-Pleaser to Purpose-Led"

## Components of a Premium Spiritual Coaching Package

Core elements include:

- 1:1 coaching sessions (60–90 minutes)
- Asynchronous support via Voxer, WhatsApp, or email
- Custom spiritual practices (meditations, breathwork, rituals)
- Resource library with workbooks and teachings
- Welcome and closing ceremonies

## FAQ

**Q:** How much should I charge?
A: Pricing reflects transformation value. New coaches: $800–$1,500 for 4-week packages; $3,000–$5,000+ for 12-week programs.

**Q:** What should 12 weeks include?
A: Regular 1:1 sessions, between-session support, custom practices, clear curriculum, and bonus offerings like readings or ceremonies.

**Q:** How do I transition from hourly to packages?
A: Identify common transformations from current clients, package into a 3-month container, notify existing clients of the shift, and update marketing materials.

**Q:** What's the difference from regular life coaching?
A: Spiritual packages incorporate energy work, intuitive guidance, meditation, ritual, and soul-level exploration alongside traditional coaching.

**Q:** How many clients simultaneously?
A: Typically 5–15 clients depending on support depth. High-touch packages: 5–8 clients. Limited support: 12–15 clients.
